PLACE YOUR IMAGES HERE
======================

This folder is served at the site root ("/").

Required (optional) images — drop them in this folder with these exact names:

1. profile.jpg   -> Your professional photo for the HERO section (right side).
                    Recommended: portrait orientation, ~800x1000px.

2. about.jpg     -> An image for the ABOUT section (e.g. your workspace / setup).
                    Recommended: square, ~800x800px.

3. resume.pdf    -> Your resume. The "Download Resume" buttons link to /resume.pdf.

Until you add profile.jpg / about.jpg, the site shows a clean styled
placeholder automatically — nothing breaks.
